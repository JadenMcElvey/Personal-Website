Portfolio Notes

  Personally I'm aiming to make good photos in a few genres. My aspirations are to create great landscape, astro, and nature photos. While these genres represent my current direction I'm not limiting myself to these types of photos. No matter what I need to produce quality photographs. This is my one goal. That said I may in time spread out and take different types of photographs. The one commitement I'm making in regards to this portfolio is to make sure all photos are of a high quality. That's the goal of my photography and that's what I will strive to have showcased here.
